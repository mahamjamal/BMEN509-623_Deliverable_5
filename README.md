## Overview

This deliverable covers nuclear medicine physics, gamma camera imaging, SPECT, and PET. You will analyze radioactive decay, explore detector physics, investigate coincidence detection in PET, and examine clinical applications of nuclear imaging.

## Getting Started

1. **Read the instructions**: Open `deliverable-05-instructions.pdf` for complete assignment details, learning objectives, and submission requirements.

2. **Complete the starter notebook**: Open `deliverable-05-starter.ipynb` in Jupyter and work through the exercises. The notebook contains scaffolded code cells and questions to guide your analysis.

3. **Explore the data**: The `data/` folder contains nuclear medicine images, decay data, and detector specifications needed for the exercises.

4. **Submit your work**: Upload your completed notebook to D2L by the due date.
